About Us

We the team of web toddlers are going to present " Project Apsis " a web-based START Up Model aimed at mapping INDIA's social as well as infrastructural growth, through real-time tracking providing access and awareness to ordinary citizens, in turn creating an ecosystem for social upliftment and employment creation. The website we have created is based on the domain of serving the people of India with adequate information about their state, city, district and even village.

We will be creating a user friendly website that will provide information about developments and infrastructure and social developmental plans proposed by the govt. for the benefit of the people of that particular locality.

Our objective is to claw into the grass root level and provide vital information that a normal citizen has no access to.
